# Make MindMap Searchable on Google

## Step 1: Publish the website
Use Netlify Drop:
1. Go to Netlify Drop.
2. Drag this whole folder onto the page.
3. Netlify gives you a public link like `https://mindmap-demo.netlify.app`.

## Step 2: Replace the placeholder URL
After you get your real website link, open these files and replace:
`https://YOUR-SITE-URL.netlify.app/`
with your actual URL:
- `index.html`
- `robots.txt`
- `sitemap.xml`

## Step 3: Submit to Google
1. Open Google Search Console.
2. Add your website URL.
3. Verify ownership.
4. Submit sitemap: `/sitemap.xml`.

## Step 4: Test indexing
After some time, search Google like:
`site:your-site-url.netlify.app`

Google can take a few hours to a few days to show new websites.
